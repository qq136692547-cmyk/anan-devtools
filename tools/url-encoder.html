URL Encoder / Decoder

Percent-encode and decode URLs. Supports full URL encoding and component-level encoding.

•100% client-side•no tracking•last updated 2026-06-24

encodeURI vs encodeURIComponent

encodeURI is for full URLs and preserves URL syntax characters like /, ?, &, #.

encodeURIComponent is for individual components and escapes all reserved characters.
